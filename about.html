About Balloon Coverage Calculator

This site exists because "how many balloons do I need?" turns out to be a genuinely hard question - and the person who inspired it builds balloon arches, garlands, and party installations and was tired of guessing. Order too few and the garland runs out three feet from the corner; order too many and the profit on the job deflates instead.

Every tool here is completely free, requires no account or signup, and runs instantly in your browser. Nothing you enter is stored or sent anywhere.

The tools:

  • Coverage Calculator - pick a shape (garland, arch, column, hoop, wall, number frame, or custom length), enter measurements, and get a balloon count broken down by size with a built-in extra-balloon buffer.
  • Pricing Calculator - for decorators: turn a balloon count into a true cost and a client quote that actually pays for your time.
  • Helium Tank Calculator - total helium volume for any mix of balloon sizes, and how many tanks that means.
  • Balloons Per Foot Chart - the quick-reference table for common garland lengths and styles.

Balloon Coverage Calculator is part of the Swrl Tools family of free calculators. Spot a count that doesn't match your real-world experience? Tell us - decorator feedback is how these ratios get better.
